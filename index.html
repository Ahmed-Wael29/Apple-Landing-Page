iPhone 13 pro max

The iPhone 13 Pro Max is a smartphone designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc. It is the fourteenth generation, high-end iPhone, succeeding the iPhone 12 Pro Max. It was unveiled at a virtually held Apple Special Event at Apple Park in Cupertino, California on September 14, 2021, alongside the iPhone 13, iPhone 13 Mini, and iPhone 13 Pro. Pre-orders for the iPhone 13 Pro Max began on September 17, 2021, and it was released on September 24, 2021.
